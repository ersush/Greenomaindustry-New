Our Services

We give High quality services

to multiple Industries.

Manufacturing.

Manufacturing Industry is one of the largest consumers of industrial gases. Industrial Gases as oxygen, nitrogen, and argon are used in metal production, welding, cutting, and brazing applications

Electronics.

The electronics industry uses gases for various applications such as semiconductor production, cleaning, and welding. Nitrogen is used for purging and as a carrier gas in the semiconductor industry.

Defence

This industry also relies heavily on industrial gases for various applications. It is used in the manufacturing and testing of weapons and ammunition, also used for purging and cleaning equipment in installations

Chemical

Chemical Industry uses industrial gases in various processes oxidation and combustion. Gases hydrogen, oxygen as well as nitrogen are used in the production of ammonia, methanol.

Food & Beverages.

This industry uses industrial gases for various applications such as freezing, carbonation, and preservation. Carbon dioxide is used for carbonating beverages, as a refrigerant, and as a preservative.

Healthcare.

This industry uses gases for various applications such as anesthesia, respiratory therapy, and cryotherapy. Oxygen is used for respiratory therapy, anesthesia, and for respiratory disorders.

Oil & Gases.

The oil and gas industry uses industrial gases for various applications such as pipeline cleaning, drilling, and refining. It is used for pipeline cleaning, as a purging gas.

Automotive

Gases such as nitrogen, oxygen, and hydrogen are used in various stages of the automotive manufacturing process. Nitrogen is used in tire inflation to ensure safety and improved fuel efficiency.
